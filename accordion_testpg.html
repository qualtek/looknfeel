FAQ ON T2 AND FIRST DIGITAL

What is T2

T2 is a new digital television technology which is an improvement on the T1 platform. It is essentially a replacement of T1 against the demands of Technological evolution.

Some Benefits of T2

T2 technology has come to provide the viewers of digital TV:
  • Clearer pictures
  • Quality sound
  • More channels on the platform
  • An electronic program guide (EPG) etc

Why T2?

As mandated by the world telecommunication convention, it is expected that by 2015 all television stations will move onto the latest standard of broadcasting (T2). In 2006, the republic of Ghana signed the Geneva 2006 agreement which established the digital terrestrial broadcasting plan to move away from the analog mode of broadcasting to a modern, convenient and innovative technology, T2.

Is First Digital on T2?

Now First Digital TV, we are joining the rest of the world on the T2 platform by commencing T2 services by the end of January 2014. Per the NCA directive, all media houses should be on the T2 digital platform by 17th June 2015. Skyy TV, a wholly Ghanaian owned company was the only TV station to operate on T1 platform since 2009.

What is Next For First Digital?

After successfully migrating to the T2 platform, in our current regions-Accra, Cape Coast, Kumasi, Takoradi, and Koforidua -our next line of action is to expand to more regions. The next phase is to cover areas that we choose to call “GOLDEN BELT”.

What is Golden Belt.

The Golden Belt is consisting of the following areas in the Western Region: *OBUASI *DAMANG *TARKWA *PRESTEA *BOGOSO *DUNKWA, ETC

We have several expansion phases planned for this year with the intention of reaching national coverage before the end of 2014.
